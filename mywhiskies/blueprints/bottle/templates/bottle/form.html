Image Guidelines

The following rules apply to all images uploaded to My Whiskies Online. Uploads that violate these guidelines will be removed, and repeated violations may result in account termination.

What you may upload

  • Photos you took yourself of whiskey bottles, whiskey glasses, or your whiskey collection
  • Photos of bottle labels, including any text or addresses appearing on the label itself

What is not permitted

  • Images you did not take or do not have rights to (no copyrighted or watermarked images)
  • Photos that include any person or recognizable face
  • Explicit, adult, or offensive content
  • Images depicting illegal activity
  • Photos containing personal information — such as handwritten notes, phone numbers, or personal addresses — that are not part of the bottle label itself
